Error Codes

  100 - Element type not supported

  20x - Material Properties ID
       
     . 201 - Unrecognized elasticity
     . 202 - Unrecognized yield function
     . 203 - Unrecognized isotropic hardening law
     . 204 - Unrecognized kinematic hardening law
     . 205 - Unrecognized uncoupled rupture criterion

  300x - Variables Size

     . 301 - Maximum allowed number of partial back stress
     . 302 - Required number of internal state variables
     . 303 - Wrong number of material properties
